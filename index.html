Services

Individual Sessions

I offer weekly or bi-weekly therapy sessions online or in-person for adults looking for long-term support and counseling.

Sessions are 50 minutes and tailored to your needs — whether that’s talking, making music, or moving through structured tools together.

I specialize in working with adults living with anxiety and depression, neurodivergent adults (ADHD, autism), people recovering from burnout, highly sensitive people, creative professionals & musicians, and adults recovering from a traumatic brain injury.

Rates: €85 individual session, €110 couple session. Sliding scale available.

Contact me to book a free 30-minute consultation.

Community Partnerships

I host ongoing groups centered on song sharing, deep listening, and open discussion, focusing on community building, group therapy, and exploring how music supports mental health.

These gatherings focus on how music can help with depression, anxiety, and emotional overwhelm — creating space to feel less alone, heard, and supported.

Held in various spaces across Berlin. Some groups are open to drop-ins, others require registration in advance.

Contact me to learn about upcoming events or to host a music therapy group.

Lessons

I offer creative, supportive music lessons for neurodivergent teens and young people, with a focus on emotional development, communication, and self-expression.

Available options include small group ensembles for teens (ages 14–17) focused on collaboration and social connection, as well as 1:1 guitar lessons approached as a therapeutic learning experience.

Contact me to learn more.
