Jordan Elias offers music therapy services in Berlin and online for adults with burnout, anxiety, and depression. Sessions are offered in a queer-affirming, neurodiversity-affirming space that centers safety, consent, and respect for each client’s lived experience. Services include individual therapy sessions, community groups and events, and adaptive guitar lessons to support mental health, emotional wellbeing, and self-expression.

Services

Individual Sessions

Weekly or bi-weekly therapy sessions tailored to your needs — talking, music-making, or structured tools and projects.

Rates: €85 individual, €110 couple. €42 students. Sliding scale available.

Contact me to book a free 30-minute consultation.

Community Partnerships

Ongoing groups focused on song sharing, deep listening, and mental health support. Build connection, explore emotions, and feel supported in a safe, creative environment.

Events held across Berlin. Some are open to drop-ins, others require advance registration.

Contact me to learn about upcoming events or hosting a group.

Lessons

Guitar lessons tailored for neurodivergent people. Options include 1:1 lessons and small group ensembles.

Contact me to learn more.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can benefit from music therapy? Adults experiencing anxiety, depression, burnout, neurodivergent individuals, musicians, or anyone seeking creative support and emotional wellbeing.
  • Do I need musical experience? No prior experience is required; sessions are tailored to your abilities and interests.
  • How can I book a session? Reach out via contact page or book a free consultation directly on Calendly.
  • Are sessions available online? Yes, all individual sessions can be conducted online securely.
