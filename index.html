Services

Individual Sessions

I offer weekly or bi-weekly therapy sessions online or in-person for adults looking for long-term support and counseling.

Sessions are 50 minutes, and tailored to your needs. That can be talking, making music, or moving through structured tools together.

I specialize in working with: Adults living with anxiety and depression, neurodivergent adults (ADHD, autism), people recovering from burnout, highly sensitive people, creative professionals & musicians, and adults recovering from a traumatic brain injury.

Rates: €85 individual session, €110 couple session. I offer a sliding scale.

Contact me to book a free 30-minute consultation.

Community Partnerships

I host ongoing groups centered on song sharing, deep listening, and open discussion, focusing on community building, group therapy, and exploring how music supports mental health.

We focus on ways music can help with depression, anxiety, and emotional overwhelm, creating space to feel less alone, heard, and supported. It’s also a chance to discover new songs.

Held in various spaces across Berlin. Some are open to drop-ins, others require registration ahead of time.

Contact me to find out about upcoming events or to host a music therapy group.

Lessons

For neurodivergent teens and young people, I offer creative spaces to explore emotional development, communication, and self-expression through music. Designed with sensory sensitivity and neurodiverse strengths in mind.

What’s Available: Small Group Ensembles for Teens (14–17) focusing on collaboration, self-expression, and social connection (8-week course with performance), and 1:1 Guitar Lessons as a therapeutic learning experience.

Contact me to learn more.
